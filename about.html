About

Background

Chronoshift Expeditions was founded in 2035, by Robert Geiger. He always supported the idea of making Time Travel accessible to more people and he wanted to accomplish that by creating a Time Safari. He started the company along with 2 scientists, but now the company has grown to a major organization with over 50 people.
